So you want to know more?

Bauble is a new web app for managing plant collections. It is intended to be used by botanic gardens, arboretums and herbariums to manage their plant records and living collections.

Our aim is to have first release in January 2014 and from there have regular incremental releases. This means that Bauble will continue to get better each week with more features.

What does this mean for you?

It means that we are developing a new way to manage plant collections. Bauble will be:

  • Mobile friendly
    Bauble will work on desktop computers, tablets and phones without extra fees and anywhere you have an internet connection.
  • Cloud-enabled
    This means that you won't need to host your own servers for your data. Let us take care of the hosting, backups, upgrades and technical support.
  • Open
    The data you keep in Bauble is yours. You can export it an anytime. You can use our REST API to integrate with other software or services like statistics, mapping or labelling.
  • International
    Bauble will be translated to multiple languages and will support your locale preferences.

It's too bad Bauble doesn't do what I've always wanted a plant record system to do :(

Don't be shy! If you wish Bauble has some fancy feature that you've always wanted in a plant record system then let us know. It might be something we're already planning. Or we might think its a great idea and put it in. It doesn't hurt to ask.

If you have any other specific questions please contact us.
